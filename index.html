ABOUT
Light is a perennial symbol of the qualities most revered by the human mind - truth, beauty, and goodness. Illumination or enlightenment - the conferring of light unto humanity and their emergence from darkness and ignorance - is considered the most exalted state attainable by humankind. The Sun - the ultimate source of light and life, has universally inspired worship, works, wonder and wisdom.
Apricity, a seventeenth century coinage, is defined as the warmth of the Sun in winter. A felicitous word by all means, Apricity is one of the most benign manifestations of the Sun, a phenomenon of pure gentleness. In desolation, the first, glimmer of hope, in slumber, the event of awakening; in intellectual barrenness, the lambent glow of inspiration; and, of the most beautiful winter.
